Essential Safety Tips for Cutting Patio Pavers

Before you start cutting patio pavers, safety should be your top priority. Cutting through stone, concrete, or brick generates dust and sharp debris that can be hazardous to your eyes, lungs, and hands. Always wear safety goggles, a dust mask or respirator, heavy-duty gloves, and hearing protection when using power tools. If you're working indoors or in a confined space, ensure proper ventilation or use a dust collection system to minimize airborne particles. Additionally, make sure your work surface is stable and that your tools are in good condition to prevent accidents.
